Special Group Effects: Drop Shadow and Transparency
Controls two advanced effects you can apply to your groups.

Drop Shadow: Draws a drop shadow surrounding the element's shape. Use the slider to set the shadow size (distance) and the color button to adjust the drop shadow's color.
Transparency: Makes the menus transparent. Moving the slider to the right makes the menus more transparent.

Compatibility notes
These effects only work on modern browsers that support CSS3.
