Timers
Allow you to customize the two delays that DHTML Menu Builder applies to the menus:

  • Root Menus Display Delay: controls the time that the mouse must rest over a hotspot or a toolbar item before the associated menu is displayed.
  • Submenus Display Delay: controls the time that the mouse must rest above a command before the command's submenu is displayed.
  • Menus Hide Delay: controls the time that the menus remain visible after the mouse is no longer over the menus.
Important Information
To ensure the proper functionality of the menus always make sure that the hide delay is greater than the display delays.
