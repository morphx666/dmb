This is the Advanced Offsets Configuration dialog.
In this dialog is where you administer the custom offsets you have defined.
To access this dialog click File->Project Properties, click on the Advanced tab, click the Menus Offset button and the click the Advanced button.

Click any part of the dialog to get information about its purpose.
