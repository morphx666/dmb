 Dialogs » Project Properties » Configurations » Frames Support

This is the frames support dialog for the Configurations section.
If you are using frames on your web site, you must configure your project in this dialog.
To access this dialog click File->Project Properties, click on the Configurations tab and then click on the Frames button.

Click any part of the dialog to get information about its purpose.

Enable Frames Support
When checked, enables frames support within DHTML Menu Builder

Frames Document
Points to the HTML document that defines your frame set.

DHTML Menu Builder will analyze your frameset and detect all the available frames in it. Then you use the list of detected frames on your links to specify the target frame where your links will be displayed.
